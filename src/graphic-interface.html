INTERFACE B Tutorial

In this ativity you will be using a multi touch interface.The red coloured panel is the place where multi touch actions need to be provided.It looks like this ...

You should touch in this area ...

There are total of six options that you can choose from.They are as follows - Pen, Pencil, Brush, Thin, Medium and Thick.The first three are different types of tracing devices whereas the latter three are different types of stroke widths.

To choose any option using the multi touch panel you need to make two touches on the panel

Hold the first touch while making the second touch

To choose an option from the types of tracing devices ie Pen , Pencil or Brush, the first touch needs to be on the left side of the screen.

Within the different types of tracing devices to choose PEN the second touch needs to be to the top of the first touch like this ...

PEN

To choose PENCIL the second touch needs to be to the right of the first touch like this ...

PENCIL

To choose BRUSH the second touch needs to be to the bottom of the first touch like this ...

BRUSH

The first touch can be anywhere on the left side of the panel it need not always be in the middle .For example both the following are actions for PEN

PEN

ALSO PEN

Also the second touch is scale independent so you can touch anywhere on the screen .So while this is pencil

PENCIL

This is also pencil

PENCIL AGAIN

To choose an option from the types of stroke widths ie Thin , Medium or Thick , the first touch needs to be on the right side of the screen.

Within the different stroke widths to choose THIN the second touch needs to be to the top of the first touch like this ...

THIN

To choose MEDIUM the second touch needs to be to the RIGHT of the first touch like this ...

MEDIUM

To choose THICK the second touch needs to be to the bottom of the first touch like this ...

THICK

Make sure the first finger is in place while performing the second touch.Go ahead and try choosing a couple of options.Once you feel you are comfortable with the interface proceed to training.
